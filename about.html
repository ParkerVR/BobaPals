About this Page

This was a page made during a BU SASE Workshop! In this workshop, we learned the basics of HTML and CSS with the assistance of Bootstrap. In addition, we also learned about the backend through the use of Flask, a Python Framework.

On this basic website, you can store you and your friend's boba order the next time you have to buy a dozen of them. It's simple product for a crazy situation.
